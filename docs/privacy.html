Privacy Policy

Aoe2Quiz · Last updated: —

Aoe2Quiz ("the app") is a free, fan-made quiz game about Age of Empires II. This page describes what data the app handles and why.

Summary

  • No accounts, no email, no phone number, no address, no location.
  • No advertising, no third-party analytics, no behavioural tracking.
  • Single-player and training modes work fully offline; nothing leaves your device.
  • Multiplayer requires a small amount of data to run matches and a leaderboard (see below).

Data stored on your device only

The app uses local on-device storage to remember:

  • Your selected language and music on/off setting.
  • Single-player progress (stars per level, total games played).
  • A locally generated device identifier (random UUID) — see below.
  • Your chosen multiplayer display name and civilization (also sent to the server when you play multiplayer).

This data is stored in the app's private storage on your device. Uninstalling the app removes it. On Android, system-level Auto Backup may copy it to your Google account if you have that feature enabled at the OS level — this is not controlled by the app.

Data sent to our server (multiplayer only)

The multiplayer mode connects to our backend (5re6.l.time4vps.cloud). Only when you use multiplayer or open the leaderboard, the following is transmitted over HTTPS / WSS:

  • Device identifier — a random UUID generated on first launch, stored on your device. It is not your IMEI, not your advertising ID, and is not linked to any other identifier. It is used to keep your rating tied to your device across matches and to deduplicate leaderboard entries. You can effectively reset it by clearing app data or reinstalling.
  • Display name and civilization icon — chosen by you. These are shown to your opponent during a match and on the public leaderboard.
  • Match data — per-question correctness flags and the final result, used to compute your rating and match outcome.
  • Network metadata — your IP address is visible to the server during the connection (this is inherent to any internet request) and may appear in temporary server logs used for abuse prevention. It is not stored long-term and is not associated with your profile.

We do not collect or store your real name, email, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone, or any other device sensors.

Public information (leaderboard)

Your chosen display name, civilization icon, and rating are visible publicly to other players on the in-app leaderboard. Do not enter personal information such as your real name, email, or phone number as your display name.

Third-party services

  • aoe2techtree.net — when the app starts it fetches a public game-data file from aoe2techtree.net (units, civilizations, tech tree). No personal data is sent to this service; it is a plain GET request for static content.
  • Google Play / Apple App Store — distribution and install reporting are handled by the platform under its own privacy policy.
  • The app does not use Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, AdMob, Facebook SDK, or any other analytics/advertising SDK at this time. If this changes in the future, this page will be updated and the change date will be reflected at the top.

Children

The app is suitable for general audiences. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will remove it.

Data retention and deletion

Local data is removed when you uninstall the app or clear its storage. For multiplayer data on the server (device identifier, display name, civilization choice, rating, match history), you can request deletion by emailing i.pavluhin12@gmail.com with the device identifier shown in the app or your in-game display name. Deletion is processed within 30 days.

Security

All network traffic between the app and the server uses HTTPS / WSS. We do not store passwords because the app has no accounts.

Changes to this policy

We may update this page from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent change. Material changes will also be communicated through an in-app notice.

Contact

Questions, requests for data deletion, or other privacy concerns: i.pavluhin12@gmail.com
