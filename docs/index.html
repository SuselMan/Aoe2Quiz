Aoe2Quiz

A fan-made quiz app about Age of Empires II.

Aoe2Quiz tests your knowledge of Age of Empires II — civilizations, units, technologies, buildings, and the tech tree. Single-player campaign, focused training mode, and ranked multiplayer with a global leaderboard.
