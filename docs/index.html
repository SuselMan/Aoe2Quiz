Privacy Policy — Aoe2Quiz

Last updated: January 2025

Aoe2Quiz is a free educational quiz game. This application does not require account registration and does not collect personally identifiable information.

Information Collection and Use

The app itself does not collect personal data such as name, email address, phone number, or location.

However, the application uses standard third-party services provided by the Android platform that may collect anonymous technical information:

  • device type
  • operating system version
  • crash logs
  • basic diagnostic information

This data is used only to improve stability and performance.

Multiplayer

If you use multiplayer features, the game may store:

  • nickname
  • rating (ELO)
  • match results

This information is public inside the game and is required for the functionality of the multiplayer mode.

External Links

The app may contain links to external websites (such as donation platforms). These websites operate under their own privacy policies and the app does not control them.

Data Sharing

The app does not sell, trade, or transfer personal data to third parties.

Children’s Privacy

The application is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated in the future. Changes will be posted on this page.
